An interactive quiz game about the rise and fall of Ancient Greece. Test your knowledge of Greek history through multiple difficulty levels and historical periods.

## Features

- **Multiple Difficulty Levels**: Easy, Medium, and Hard
- **Historical Periods**: 
  - The Rise of Greece (Minoan & Mycenaean civilizations)
  - Golden Age (Classical period, Persian Wars, Peloponnesian War)
  - Decline & Fall (Macedonian conquest, Hellenistic period, Roman conquest)
- **Character Selection**: Choose from 5 unique heroes with special abilities
- **Power-Ups**: Use hints, strikes, time freezes, and double points
- **Scoring System**: Points based on difficulty, streak, and time remaining
- **Progress Tracking**: Levels, stages, and high scores saved locally

## How to Play

1. Select your difficulty level
2. Choose a historical period (or all periods)
3. Pick your hero character
4. Answer questions correctly to earn points
5. Use power-ups strategically
6. Try to beat your high score!

## Game Files

- `index.html` - Main game interface
- `game.js` - Game logic and question database
- `style.css` - Styling and animations

## Play the Game

Simply open `index.html` in your web browser to start playing!
